aboutabout myself

Welcome to my world of software development! I'm thrilled to share my journey and showcase the projects that define my commitment to excellence.

Welcome to my portfolio website! With over 20 years of experience in the software development realm, I am a seasoned professional dedicated to crafting innovative solutions that push the boundaries of technology.


My journey began in 2002 when I enrolled as a Computer Science student at the University of KwaZulu-Natal, graduating in 2004. I later furthered my Computer Science studies through the University of South Africa, solidifying my foundation and expanding my expertise.


What started as a passion for coding has evolved into a thrilling adventure, enabling me to work on diverse projects and master a wide array of skills. My expertise spans various programming languages, including Python, Django, C++, and Qt, empowering me to create robust and efficient applications. Additionally, I am well-versed in front-end technologies such as JavaScript, JQuery, and HTML5, ensuring seamless user experiences and captivating interfaces. Database management is also one of my strengths, with proficiency in Postgres and MySQL, allowing me to build and maintain data-driven applications effectively.


Throughout my career, I have collaborated with clients from different industries, from startups to established enterprises, transforming ideas into successful projects. My work portfolio showcases a diverse range of applications, web platforms, and software solutions that highlight my commitment to excellence and my ability to adapt to various challenges.
